Free Online Passport Photo Maker

This passport size photo maker by Ranjit Studio Kapka helps you create printable passport photo sheets from a single image. You can upload your photo, choose the number of copies, select paper size, and download the output as a PDF.

How to Use This Tool

Upload your image, enter how many photos you want, choose A4 or Letter paper size, and click the generate button. The tool will place multiple passport size photos on the page and download a printable PDF file.

Useful For

This tool is useful for passport photos, ID photos, school forms, job applications, and document submissions. It works directly in your browser without needing extra software.
